How it Works

In the form below, you can input a piece of text in one of four languages and the analyzer will translate it and understand its sentiment score. The score ranges from -1.0 to 1.0 with a negative score indicating a negative sentence and a positive score indicating a positive sentence. Now go on and give it a try!

Supported Languages: English, Spanish, French, and Chinese
